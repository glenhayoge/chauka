# Creating Your Account

To use Chauka, you need an account. There are two ways to get started: registering yourself or accepting an invitation from your organisation.

## Self-registration

1. Go to the Chauka home page and click **Get started** (or navigate directly to the registration page)
2. Fill in your details:
   - **Full name** — your display name within Chauka
   - **Email address** — used for sign-in and notifications
   - **Password** — choose a strong password (minimum 8 characters)
3. Click **Create account**
4. You'll be signed in and taken to the organisation selection screen

After registering, you can either create a new organisation or wait for an invitation to join an existing one.

## Accepting an invitation

If your organisation already uses Chauka, an administrator can invite you by email.

1. Check your inbox for an invitation email from Chauka
2. Click the **Accept Invitation** link in the email
3. If you already have an account, sign in — you'll be added to the organisation automatically
4. If you don't have an account, you'll be prompted to create one, and then you'll join the organisation

## Signing in

Once you have an account:

1. Go to the sign-in page
2. Enter your **email** and **password**
3. Click **Sign in**

You'll be taken to the organisation selection screen if you belong to multiple organisations, or directly to your logframe list if you belong to one.

## Forgot your password?

1. On the sign-in page, click **Forgot password?**
2. Enter the email address associated with your account
3. Check your inbox for a password reset link
4. Click the link and choose a new password

The reset link expires after a limited time. If it has expired, request a new one.
