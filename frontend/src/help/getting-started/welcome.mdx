# Welcome to Chauka

Chauka is a **logframe management platform** built for development organisations, NGOs, and M&E professionals. It helps you plan, monitor, and report on your programmes using the logical framework (logframe) approach.

## What Chauka does

Chauka gives your team a shared workspace where you can:

- **Design result chains** — build hierarchical logframes with Impacts, Outcomes, and Outputs
- **Track indicators** — define indicators with sub-indicators, set targets across reporting periods, and record actual data
- **Manage activities** — plan activities, milestones, and timelines linked to your results
- **Monitor budgets** — track budget lines and expenses against your work plan
- **Collaborate** — invite team members with role-based permissions (lead, data collector, viewer)
- **Export reports** — generate Excel exports for donor reporting and internal review

## How Chauka is organised

Chauka uses a hierarchy to keep your work structured:

| Level | What it represents |
|---|---|
| **Organisation** | Your NGO, agency, or company |
| **Programme** | A thematic area or funding stream |
| **Project** | A specific funded initiative |
| **Logframe** | The results framework for a project |

Inside each logframe, you build a **results tree** — a hierarchy of what you're trying to achieve — and then attach indicators, activities, and budgets to track progress.

## Who is Chauka for?

- **M&E Officers** tracking indicators and collecting data
- **Programme Managers** overseeing results across projects
- **Project Leads** managing activities, budgets, and team workload
- **Data Collectors** entering field data against indicators
- **Senior Management** reviewing dashboards and progress reports
