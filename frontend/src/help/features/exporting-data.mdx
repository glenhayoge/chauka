# Exporting Data

Chauka lets you export your logframe data to Excel for donor reporting, offline analysis, and sharing with stakeholders who don't have Chauka access.

## What you can export

Exports include:

- **Results framework** — the full results tree with descriptions
- **Indicator data** — targets and actuals for all indicators across reporting periods
- **Activity data** — activities, milestones, and their status
- **Budget data** — budget lines and expenses

## How to export

1. Open the logframe you want to export
2. Look for the **Export** option (typically in the logframe menu or toolbar)
3. Choose the export format (Excel)
4. The file will download to your computer

## What the Excel export contains

The export generates a structured spreadsheet with:

### Results sheet
- Result hierarchy (Impact, Outcome, Output)
- Result descriptions and numbering
- Assumptions and risk ratings

### Indicators sheet
- Indicator names and descriptions
- Sub-indicators
- Target values by reporting period
- Actual values by reporting period
- Variance calculations

### Activities sheet
- Activity descriptions
- Milestone dates and status
- Timeline information

### Budget sheet
- Budget lines by activity
- Budgeted amounts
- Actual expenses by period
- Remaining balances

## Using exports for donor reporting

Many donors require periodic progress reports in a specific format. Chauka exports give you the raw data, which you can then:

1. **Copy into donor templates** — transfer indicator data into the required format
2. **Create charts** — use Excel's chart features to visualise progress
3. **Share with partners** — send the export to consortium partners or sub-grantees
4. **Archive** — keep periodic snapshots for your records

## Tips

- **Export regularly** — create periodic snapshots (e.g., quarterly) for your records
- **Check data before exporting** — ensure all data entries are up to date
- **Label your files** — include the logframe name and date in the filename
- **Compare exports over time** — track how indicators have progressed between periods

## Who can export?

All roles can view data, but export access is typically available to:
- Organisation Admins
- Project Leads

Check with your administrator if you need export access.
